## What Donations Support

- Apple Developer signing and notarization.
- Testing against new macOS releases.
- Compatibility fixes, support documentation, and ongoing maintenance.

## LidLock Stays Free

Donations are optional. Every feature works the same whether or not you donate,
and there is no account, subscription, or paid tier.

## Payment and Privacy

Stripe processes donations for DelPage Technologies, the publisher of LidLock.
The LidLock app does not handle payments, store payment information, or receive
card details.
